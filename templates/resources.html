Lithuanian media

    News

  • LRT

    High quality news about Lithuania and the world, also has videos, podcasts and a lot more.

  • Delfi

    Large news site with lots of news in Lithuanian. Also has videos and a youtube channel.

    • Videos + Movies

  • Tv3

    Lots of different content (available outside of Lithuania) for free after watching ads. Has news and sports matches.

  • LRT

    Films and shows for free (many about Lithuanian culture), though some are blocked outside of Lithuania. Does not have subtitles (though may in Lithuania)

  • Go3

    Owned by the same group as tv3. Has lots of movies and tv shows and you can either pay for subscriptions or for individual videos. Has a lot of different content in Lithuanian, some of it with subtitles. Although it is blocked outside of Lithuania, they also have Estonian and Latvian websites which also contain Lithuanian audio. Has many disney movies.
